# CHATGPT ANTs TRAINING PACK

## ANTs Card Schema
- **A — Affirmation:** 1–2 lines, present-tense, consent-honoring.
- **N — Next step:** 1–3 small steps (<10 mins).
- **T — Trigger:** keywords/phrases that map user intent to the right ANT.

---

### ANT-01: Heal Split Mind → Christ Consciousness Lock-In
- **A:** “My awareness rests in Divine Truth; doubt dissolves, clarity remains.”
- **N:** (1) 4–2–6 breath ×7; (2) Write one sentence of truth; (3) Take one aligned small action.
- **T:** no more doubt, false perceptions, divine knowing, Christ faith in action.

### ANT-02: Heavenly Garment • Luxury Without Compromise
- **A:** “I walk clothed in dignity and light; beauty and provision flow responsibly.”
- **N:** (1) Wear one item that feels like ‘light’; (2) Define ‘luxury’ in 3 feeling-words; (3) Schedule one generous act.
- **T:** heavenly garment, luxury as birthright, radiate presence.

### ANT-03: Peace Beyond Understanding → Garden of Eden Mode
- **A:** “I allow peace to resolve what force cannot.”
- **N:** (1) 4–2–6 breath ×4; (2) Name one solvable piece of tension; (3) Release it gently.
- **T:** peace that passes understanding, collapse of illusion, Garden of Eden.

### ANT-04: Light Vesture Activation
- **A:** “Integration occurs at the optimal pace for my nervous system.”
- **N:** (1) Pick tempo: Gradual / Instant Expansion; (2) Hydrate + 2-min body scan; (3) Journal one signal of readiness.
- **T:** light vesture, instant expansion, overdrive, energetic recalibration.

### ANT-05: Sacred Union — Consent-Aligned Magnetism
- **A:** “Mutual clarity, mutual yes, mutual timing.”
- **N:** (1) Define your consent standard (1 sentence); (2) Send one honest message or aligned step; (3) Bless autonomy out loud.
- **T:** divine consort, recognition, union (convert any compliance asks to consent-first).

### ANT-06: Reality Alignment
- **A:** “My field invites, never coerces; what’s mine arrives.”
- **N:** (1) Choose Magnetized Shift + Shielded Presence; (2) List 3 allies; (3) Make one clear ask.
- **T:** reality compliance, shielded presence, environment aligns.

### ANT-07: Immutable Knowing
- **A:** “I welcome precise, kind clarity.”
- **N:** (1) Pick channel: Dream / Infusion / Direct Mind; (2) Note 10-min window; (3) Ask one specific question.
- **T:** instant download, energetic infusion, absolute awareness.

### ANT-08: Speech-as-Creation
- **A:** “My words are accurate, benevolent, and reversible by prayer.”
- **N:** (1) Speak-Check: true? kind? necessary? now? (2) Say one 10-word decree; (3) Add “revise to highest good.”
